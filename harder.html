About Us

BingBox store in Jeddah

At Bingbox Snow Cream Co., we represent an innovative take on modern Korean shaved ice (bingsu), revolutionizing it by infusing flavors into the snow itself, offering a unique ice base of mango, matcha, chocolate, and other distinctive tastes Using only quality ingredients, our snow cream is a healthier, lighter alternative to traditional ice cream or frozen yogurt, resulting in the finest and purest snowflakes. Our unique flavor mixtures and combining with state-of-the-art instant freeze technology means you won't find another dessert like Bingbox anywhere else!

The meaning of ‘bing box’ B.I. is: bright, soft, young, sensual dessert. The bing box is designed to image of a brand that wants to offer a variety of flavors and happiness. Also the bright and vivid colors to catch in the eye easily in anywhere.
